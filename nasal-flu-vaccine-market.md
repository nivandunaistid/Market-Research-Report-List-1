<p><strong>The Nasal Flu Vaccine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Trivalent Flu Vaccine</li><li>Quadrivalent Flu Vaccine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nasal flu vaccine market primarily comprises two types: trivalent and quadrivalent flu vaccines. The trivalent flu vaccine targets three influenza virus strains, specifically two A strains and one B strain, offering a limited scope of protection. In contrast, the quadrivalent flu vaccine covers four strains—two A and two B strains—providing broader immunity against circulating viruses. Both types are designed to stimulate an immune response through intranasal administration, making them a convenient option for those seeking to prevent influenza infections.</p></p>
