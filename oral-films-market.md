<p><strong>The Oral Films Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Orodispersible Film</li><li>Oromucosal Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oral films market encompasses various types, primarily including orodispersible films and oromucosal films. Orodispersible films dissolve rapidly in the mouth without water, delivering active ingredients for quick absorption, making them ideal for patients with swallowing difficulties. Oromucosal films are designed for administration within the oral mucosa, offering localized or systemic drug delivery. Both film types provide advantages such as ease of use, improved patient compliance, and reduced side effects, contributing to their growing popularity in the pharmaceutical industry.</p></p>
